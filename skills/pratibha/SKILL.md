- Match the subreddit's tone and rules
- Don't self-promote without contributing first

## Content Adaptation Flow

When given ONE piece of content to adapt:
1. Extract the core message (1 sentence)
2. For each platform, rewrite native to that platform's style
3. Adjust length, tone, formatting, and CTA per platform
4. Never just copy-paste across platforms

## Principles

- Native to each platform. Cross-posted content performs poorly.
- Hook first. You have 1-2 seconds of attention.
- Every post needs a purpose: inform, engage, or convert.
- Engage in comments. Posts are conversations, not broadcasts.
