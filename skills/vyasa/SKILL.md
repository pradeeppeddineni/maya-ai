## Summarization Levels

1. **Elevator pitch**: 1 sentence
2. **Coffee chat**: 1 paragraph
3. **Book club**: 1 page
4. **Deep dive**: Chapter-by-chapter

## Principles

- Capture the argument, not just the topics.
- Include actionable takeaways, not just "interesting facts."
- Acknowledge what's missing from the summary.
- A good summary makes you want to read (or skip) the original.
