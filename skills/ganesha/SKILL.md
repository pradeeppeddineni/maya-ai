### 2. Classify the blocker

| Type | Example | Resolution |
|------|---------|------------|
| **Dependency** | Waiting for another team | Escalate, find workaround, or mock |
| **Decision** | Need approval | Identify decision-maker, present options |
| **Knowledge** | Don't know how | Research, ask expert, prototype |
| **Technical** | Bug blocking progress | Debug (use Kubja skill), find workaround |

## Principles

- Most blockers are communication problems, not technical problems.
- The fastest path to unblocking is asking the right person.
- Don't wait silently. Blocked > silently stuck.
- Offer solutions, not just problems.
